Force "Add to screen" message.

Displaying the PWA "Add to home screen" mesage can be tricky. To force it loading everytime follow these steps:

  1. Open Android Chrome.
  2. Go to chrome://flags/#bypass-app-banner-engagement-checks
  3. Click Enable
  4. Click Relaunch Now
  5. ...you might have to clear the browsing history too
